Effective Date: May 14, 2026
Application: BandScreen for iOS (iOS 18.0 and later)

Summary

BandScreen is a peer-to-peer screen sharing app. It does not connect to the internet, does not use any servers, and does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device and on your local network. Subscription payments are handled entirely by Apple; BandScreen never sees your payment information.

This Privacy Policy and Terms of Use ("Policy") governs your use of the BandScreen iOS application (the "App"). "BandScreen," "we," "us," or "our" refers to the publisher of the App as identified on the App Store listing.

By installing or using the App, you confirm you are of legal age in your jurisdiction (or a minor with parental consent) and agree to be bound by this Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

BandScreen does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. This includes names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, payment information, or the content of any Broadcast. The App contains no code that uploads any such data to BandScreen or any third party. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or advertising libraries in the App.

3. Information Processed Locally

The following is processed on your device only and is never sent to BandScreen:

  • Screen frames: When you act as Host, iOS supplies screen frames to the App. These are compressed and transmitted over the Local Network to your chosen Viewers, processed entirely in volatile memory, and never written to disk.
  • Room code and settings: Stored in a sandboxed on-device container and deleted when you delete the App.
  • Quick messages: Host-to-Viewer text messages are queued briefly on-device and transmitted over the Local Network. They are not stored after delivery.
  • Subscription state: Apple's system supplies your subscription status (product ID, expiry date). BandScreen does not receive your payment details.

4. Local Network & Peer Discovery

The App uses Apple's standard local network peer discovery. iOS will prompt you for local network permission on first use. Your chosen display name may be visible to other devices on the same network running the App. Do not use a display name that reveals sensitive information.

5. No Internet, No Servers

The App does not communicate with any BandScreen-controlled server. The only network traffic that may leave your local network is initiated by Apple itself (App Store, in-app purchase validation), governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

6. Subscriptions & Payments

Hosting features require an in-app subscription. Viewer use is always free. Subscriptions are sold through the Apple App Store. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Refund requests must be submitted to Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.
