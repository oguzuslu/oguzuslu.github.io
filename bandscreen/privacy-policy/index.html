Summary

BandScreen is a peer-to-peer screen sharing app. It does not connect to the internet, does not use any servers, and does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. Everything happens on your device and on your local network. Subscription payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store; BandScreen never sees your payment information.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

BandScreen does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. This includes names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, payment information, or the content of any Broadcast or message. The App contains no code that uploads any such data to BandScreen or any third party. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics libraries, or advertising libraries in the App.

3. Information Processed Locally

The following is processed on your device only and is never sent to BandScreen:

  • Screen frames: When you act as Host, iOS supplies screen frames to the App. These are compressed and transmitted over the Local Network to your chosen Viewers, processed entirely in volatile memory (RAM), and are never written to permanent disk storage or retained after delivery.
  • Room code and settings: The App stores your room code, display name, viewer limit, and connected viewer list in a sandboxed on-device container. This data is deleted when you delete the App.
  • Quick messages: Host-to-Viewer text messages are queued briefly on-device and transmitted over the Local Network. They are not stored after delivery.
  • Subscription state: Apple's in-app purchase system supplies your subscription status (product ID, expiry date) to the App. BandScreen does not receive your name, email, billing address, or payment details.

4. Local Network & Peer Discovery

The App uses Apple's standard local network peer discovery. iOS will prompt you for local network permission on first use. Your chosen display name may be visible to other devices on the same network running the App. Do not use a display name that reveals sensitive information.

Performance Disclaimer: As transmission occurs strictly over your Local Network (P2P), the quality, frame rate, and stability of the broadcast are directly linked to the performance of your local network hardware and environmental interference. BandScreen is not liable for performance issues caused by your network infrastructure.

5. No Internet, No Servers

The App does not communicate with any BandScreen-controlled server. The only network traffic that may leave your local network is initiated by Apple itself (App Store, in-app purchase validation, system diagnostics), governed by Apple's own privacy policy, not ours.
