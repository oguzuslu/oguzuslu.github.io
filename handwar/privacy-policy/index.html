Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 21, 2026

This privacy policy is applicable to the Hand War: Rock Paper Scissors mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was created by Oğuz Uslu (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as a Commercial service.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to be used without any user registration or login. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (such as name, email, or phone number).

  • Gameplay Data: All game logic and results (Rock, Paper, Scissors outcomes) are processed locally on your device.
  • In-App Purchases: The Application offers "Non-Consumable" digital products, such as premium hand designs and sound effects. All financial transactions are handled securely by Apple's App Store (StoreKit). We do not have access to your payment information or credit card details.

2. Third-Party Access

The Service Provider does not use any third-party services (such as analytics, advertising networks, or external databases) that collect data. Your interaction with the Application remains strictly private on your device.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

The Application does not use "cookies" explicitly. However, it uses local storage (UserDefaults) to save your purchased hand designs and sound preferences on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Children’s Privacy

The Application is rated 9+ and is designed to be safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with any data, please contact us.

5. Security

We value your trust. Since the Application does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised from our side. We rely on the security protocols of the iOS operating system to keep your local data and purchases safe.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any updates are effective immediately after being posted on this page.
